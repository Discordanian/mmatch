Movement Match

Have you been upset about the election?

Here's something you can do.


People are imperfect. Organizations are imperfect. Movements are imperfect.

​Now is not the time for perfectionism.
Now is the time to find your group, hunker down, and start planning the long game.


This project started as a small group trying to brainstorm about these issues:

1. Trump just got elected. A lot of things have already been hard, but it's going to get worse.

2. New people are fired up and ready to fight the Trump administration! We need ways to direct this new energy and power.

3. We need to fight complacency and normalization. We need ways for people to stay involved after inauguration.

4. We need to be ready for a long fight. How will we get this amazing mass of newly energized people trained and ready to fight for the long haul?

5. We don't need to start from scratch. How can we support all of the amazing organizations who have already been in this fight for the long haul?

#MovementMatch was born from these questions.

One thing we know for sure is that there are many groups and organizations that are ready to provide the training, community, and accountability that we’ll need to stay strong for the work ahead.

Our goal is to connect more people to existing activist powerhouses before the inauguration to further build the strength of our movement and fight isolation, alienation, and complacency.
